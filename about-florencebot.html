Meet FlorenceBot Pro

Your AI-powered nursing study companion living in Discord

What is FlorenceBot?

FlorenceBot is an advanced Discord bot specifically designed for nursing students. Get instant access to clinical references, lab values, medication information, and NCLEX prep materials right in your Discord server.

Why Discord?

Discord is where nursing students collaborate, study together, and support each other. FlorenceBot brings professional clinical references directly into this environment, making it easier than ever to access critical information while studying with peers.

Instant Responses

Get lab values, vital signs, and medication info in seconds with simple slash commands.

Study with Friends

Access FlorenceBot in your study group servers for collaborative learning.

Always Available

24/7 access to clinical references whenever you need them, day or night.
