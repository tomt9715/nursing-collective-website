About The Nursing Collective

Built by nursing students, for nursing students.

Our Story

The Nursing Collective started because we know how overwhelming nursing school can be. Between clinical rotations, stacks of textbooks, and high-stakes exams, finding clear and organized study materials shouldn't be another source of stress.

We created this platform to give nursing students the study tools we wished we had — well-organized, clinically relevant, and designed to actually help you understand the material, not just memorize it.

Every guide is built around how nurses actually think through patient care, so you're not just studying for exams — you're building the clinical reasoning skills you'll use every day.

Our Mission

We believe every nursing student deserves access to high-quality study materials that make complex topics click. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and clinical practice with guides that are clear, comprehensive, and built around real nursing priorities.

What We Offer

Study Guides

Comprehensive, clinically-focused guides covering every major nursing topic from cardiovascular to mental health.

FlorenceBot

Our AI-powered study assistant on Discord that helps you review concepts, quiz yourself, and prepare for exams.

Community

A supportive Discord community where nursing students help each other, share tips, and stay motivated together.

Always Improving

Our guides are regularly updated based on student feedback and the latest evidence-based practice guidelines.
