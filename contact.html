Refund Policy for Digital Products

Eligible for Full Refund

Subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of purchase if you are not satisfied. Contact us for a hassle-free refund.

After Download - All Sales Final

Once content has been downloaded or accessed, all sales are final. Digital products cannot be "returned" like physical items, so we are unable to offer refunds after the content has been accessed. Please review product descriptions and sample content before purchasing.

Exceptions We Review

We understand things happen. We'll review refund requests on a case-by-case basis for: technical issues preventing access, accidental duplicate subscriptions, or billing errors. Contact us with your order details and we'll work with you to find a solution.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and returned to your original payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is initiated.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a refund?

Email us at support@thenursingcollective.pro with your account email and subscription details and the reason for your refund request. We'll respond within 24-48 hours.

I can't access my study guides. What should I do?

First, make sure you're logged into the same account you used to subscribe. Check your Dashboard for your study guides. If you still have issues, contact us with your order details and we'll help resolve it.

Can I get a preview before purchasing?

Yes! Each study guide includes a description and topic list on its product page. We also offer individual guides so you can try one before purchasing a bundle.

How long do I have access to my guides?

Access depends on your subscription plan. Monthly and semester plans provide access for their duration, while lifetime plans include permanent access, including any future updates to that content.
