                              CARDIOVASCULAR (6)
===============================================================================

HEART FAILURE (heart-failure)
1. Identify left vs right-sided heart failure symptoms
2. Differentiate systolic and diastolic dysfunction
3. Prioritize nursing interventions for fluid overload

MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION (myocardial-infarction)
1. Recognize STEMI vs NSTEMI presentation
2. Interpret cardiac biomarkers (troponin, CK-MB)
3. Understand tPA administration criteria

ARRHYTHMIAS (arrhythmias)
1. Interpret common ECG rhythms
2. Identify life-threatening dysrhythmias
3. Know when to defibrillate vs cardiovert

HYPERTENSION (hypertension)
1. Classify hypertension stages accurately
2. Teach lifestyle modifications effectively
3. Recognize hypertensive crisis signs

CORONARY ARTERY DISEASE (coronary-artery-disease)
1. Explain stable vs unstable angina
2. Identify modifiable risk factors
3. Prepare patients for cardiac catheterization

PERIPHERAL VASCULAR DISEASE (peripheral-vascular-disease)
1. Differentiate arterial vs venous insufficiency
2. Assess ankle-brachial index (ABI)
3. Implement DVT prevention strategies
