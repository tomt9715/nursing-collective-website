Everything you need to know about our subscription plans

Every subscription gives you unlimited access to all our content: 50+ detailed study guides across 10 clinical topics, NCLEX-style quiz bank, clinical confidence resources, quick reference tools, and nursing school survival guides. You also get access to any new content we release during your subscription.

AI-Powered plans include everything in our Standard plans, plus AI study tools. Upload your own lecture notes and slides, and our AI will generate condensed review sheets, NCLEX-style practice questions tailored to your material, and a gap analysis showing what your notes might be missing. It's like having a personal study assistant.

Standard plans give you full access to all our content:

Monthly ($14.99/mo): Great if you want to try it out or only need access for a short time.
Semester ($44.99): 4 months of full access at a 25% discount. Great for focused study periods.
Lifetime ($149): One payment, access forever. Perfect if you're early in your program.

AI-Powered plans include everything above plus AI study tools that analyze your own notes, generate NCLEX-style practice questions, and identify gaps in your knowledge.
