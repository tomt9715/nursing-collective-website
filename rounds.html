Welcome to Rounds

Clinical reasoning, gamified.

Each scenario plays out like a bedside shift handoff:

  1. Pick a patient scenario. Specialty and difficulty are shown up front.
  2. Read their chart and chief complaint. Vitals, allergies, code status, PMHx, meds — everything you'd have at the bedside.
  3. Ask questions from a limited budget. Every question gets a real answer — but not every question helps. Pick what would actually narrow the diagnosis.
  4. Commit to your diagnosis and priority nursing action.
  5. See the debrief. What was actually going on, which questions were high-yield, and why this action over the others.

Stars come from getting the diagnosis right, the action right, and asking efficient questions.
