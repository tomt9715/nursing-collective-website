Delete Your Account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Deleting your account will:

  • Remove all your profile information and profile picture
  • Cancel your plan and revoke access to all study guides
  • Delete your billing history and download records
  • Remove any remaining AI credits and usage history
  • Remove any connected accounts (Discord, Google)
  • Clear your notification preferences

Important: Make sure to download any guides before deleting your account. You will lose access to all guides and AI features immediately upon deletion.
